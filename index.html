    WELCOME TO FOUNTAIN UNIVERSITY

    A pace-setter in learning, character-building
    and service to humanity

    Fountain University was granted an operational license as a Private University on May 17, 2007, by the Federal Government on the recommendations of the National Universities Commission. Following this achievement, a Strategic Implementation Committee was set up to serve as “in loco Council” to actualize the birth of the University. This Committee worked tirelessly to put in place the necessary facilities for the University to take off

    The desire of the Nasrul-Lahi-Il-Fatih Society (NASFAT) to establish a University was borne out of its educational policy and plan articulated at its strategic Retreat held at Akodo, Lagos, in the year 2000. From this humble beginning, the Society started a systematic process which led to the hosting of an academic summit of 30 Distinguished Academic Personalities from where an 18-person University Planning Committee emerged in January 2004.

    Fountain University has since grown into a beacon of academic excellence and moral uprightness, rooted in its founding principles of knowledge, character, and service. The institution remains committed to nurturing well-rounded graduates equipped not only with intellectual skills but also with ethical values essential for national development and global competitiveness. With an inclusive and innovation-driven learning environment, the University continues to expand its academic programs, research capacity, and community engagement efforts to fulfill its mission of transforming lives through quality education.

    Professor Olayinka Ramota Karim

    Our Program

    what we offer

    Undergraduate Degree

    Fountain University offers a range of accredited undergraduate degree programs designed to equip students with academic excellence, practical skills, and strong moral values for success in their chosen careers.

    Postgraduate Degree

    Fountain University’s postgraduate programs provide advanced academic and professional training, fostering research, innovation, and leadership to prepare graduates for impactful roles in academia, industry, and society.

    Sub-Degree

    Fountain University’s sub-degree programs offer foundational knowledge and practical skills, serving as a stepping stone for further education or immediate entry into the workforce.
